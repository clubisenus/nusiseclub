ISE Day

Held on the last friday of school right before Recess Week, ISE undergraduates take a break from lessons and gather for an afternoon of food and chilling! ISE Day is an annual two-part event. The first part consists of informative talks regarding VIP, SEP and NOC followed by a feedback and Q&A session with professors from the ISEM department. The second part is a get together sessions with food, games, giveaways, lucky draw and even performances!
